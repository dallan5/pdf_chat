"Exercise offers numerous **health benefits**:

- **Improved cardiovascular health**: Engaging in aerobic activities strengthens the heart.
- **Increased muscle strength**: Lifting weights or bodyweight exercises can enhance muscle tone.
- *Flexibility*: Practices like yoga and pilates improve joint and muscle flexibility.
- **Enhanced mood**: Physical activity releases endorphins which can help in elevating mood and reducing stress.
- Better sleep: Regular exercise can improve sleep patterns and quality.
